# Ion Drift — Color Scheme

## Primary Colors

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Primary Ion Blue | Electric plasma blue | `#2FA4FF` |
| Secondary Signal Cyan | Telemetry signal | `#00E5FF` |
| Accent Ion Violet | Advanced analytics / highlights | `#7A5CFF` |
| Energy Magenta | Alerts / spikes | `#FF4FD8` |
